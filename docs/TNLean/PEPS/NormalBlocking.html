    Normal PEPS blocking hypotheses #

    The normal PEPS proof in arXiv:1804.04964 first blocks the lattice into injective regions, then applies the same three-site injective-chain argument used for injective PEPS. We record the finite-region hypotheses needed for that reduction.

    The hypotheses below do not prove that a given geometric square-lattice region is injective. Instead they make explicit the assumptions carried by the normal theorem: local rectangular injectivity, edge-centred three-region blockings, and one-site-different injective regions with injective complements.

    References #

    • Molnár, Garre-Rubio, Pérez-García, Schuch, Cirac, Normal projected entangled pair states generating the same state, arXiv:1804.04964, Section 3, Theorem 3 and Theorem normal
