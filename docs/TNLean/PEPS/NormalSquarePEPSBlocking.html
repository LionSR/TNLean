    Square-lattice normal PEPS blocking hypotheses #

    This file connects the square-lattice edge-blocking construction with the general normal PEPS blocking hypotheses.

    References #

    • [Molnár, Garre-Rubio, Pérez-García, Schuch, Cirac, Normal projected entangled pair states generating the same state, arXiv:1804.04964, Section 3, Theorem 3]

    NormalPEPSBlockingHypotheses κ (squareLatticeGraph width height)

    Oriented margin-and-cover hypotheses at every edge, together with one-site separation, assemble into the general normal PEPS blocking hypotheses.

    The edge-blocking part is the existing construction normalSquareEdgeBlockingHypotheses_of_marginCovers; the one-site separation hypothesis is carried unchanged.

    Source: arXiv:1804.04964, Section 3, proof of Theorem 3, lines 1475--1500, and theorem labelled normal, lines 1576--1583.
