    TI Reduction Corollary (Section 5, arXiv:1804.04964) #

    For constant (translation-invariant) chains (A, …, A) and (B, …, B) with A injective, SameMPV on the combined tensors gives $B^i = X A^i X^{-1}$ for a single X ∈ GL(D, ℂ), so B is injective.

    • ti_reduction_corollary — from SameMPV on combined tensors
    • ti_reduction_of_sameState — from SameState via the blocking connection hypothesis

    TI Reduction Corollary.

    If A is injective and the constant chains (A, …, A) and (B, …, B) satisfy SameMPV on their combined tensors, then B is gauge equivalent to A and B is injective.
