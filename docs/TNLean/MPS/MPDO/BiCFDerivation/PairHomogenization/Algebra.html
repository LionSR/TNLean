    Subdirect product algebra density for pair-span homogenization #

    This module contains the subdirect product algebra density argument: the algebra generated by simultaneous one-letter pairs, gauge-equivalence detection via Skolem–Noether, two-sided axis ideals, and the matrix-pair subdirect-product dichotomy. It is part of the pair-span homogenization layer for MPDO biCF.

      The unital algebra generated by simultaneous pair words of one letter.

      For equal bond dimensions this is the subdirect product algebra used in the Burnside-Jacobson branch of the BNT pair-separation argument.
