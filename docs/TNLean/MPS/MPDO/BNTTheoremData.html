    BNT-label theorem data for MPDO algebra structures #

    This file records the theorem-data layer for the BNT-label form of arXiv:1606.00608, Theorem IV.13(ii). The coefficient, operator, trace-scalar, chi, and blocked-basis comparison primitives are kept in the coefficient file; the existential witness is recorded separately.

    The declarations here do not yet construct the source objects from an MPDO tensor. They collect the objects and consequences that the Appendix C.3--C.4 argument must provide.
