    Self-overlap convergence auxiliaries #

    This module collects small auxiliary lemmas about the convergence of MPV self-overlaps used by both the BNT Permutation Rigidity argument and the Fundamental Theorem.

    Main statements #

    • tendsto_norm_selfOverlap_one: normed form of a self-overlap tending to 1.
    • tendsto_norm_mpvState_one: MPV-state norm form of a self-overlap tending to 1.

    Tags #

    matrix product states, overlap, convergence

    MPV-state norm-convergence form of normalized self-overlap convergence.

    Source: arXiv:1606.00608, Theorem thm1, Appendix MPV proof line 1182. In the block-selection contradiction, normalized BNT blocks have self-overlap tending to one; this lemma rewrites that normalization as convergence of the Hilbert space norm of the corresponding MPV state.
