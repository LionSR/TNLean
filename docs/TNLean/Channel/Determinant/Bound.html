    Determinant bounds for positive trace-preserving maps #

    This file isolates Wolf Theorem 6.1(1): a positive trace-preserving map on $M_d(\mathbb{C})$ has determinant of modulus at most $1$ when viewed as a linear endomorphism of the ambient matrix algebra.

    The proof proceeds by decomposing a trace-zero Hermitian matrix as a scalar multiple of a difference of density matrices, showing that the positive trace-preserving orbit of such a matrix stays norm-bounded, and then applying this bounded-orbit argument to eigenvectors of the channel.
