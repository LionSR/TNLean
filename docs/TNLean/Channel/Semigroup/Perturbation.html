    ℕ → TNLean.MatrixCLM (Fin D)

    Recursive Dyson-Phillips terms built from the unperturbed semigroup L and perturbation L' - L.

      Factorial norm bound for Dyson–Phillips iterates (Wolf Eq. 7.13 estimate). For s ∈ [0, t] and M = sup_{u ∈ [0,t]} ‖exp(uL)‖: ‖T̃ⁿ(s)‖ ≤ M · (s · ‖Δ‖ · M)ⁿ / n!.
