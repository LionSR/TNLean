    Cyclic projections of a finite-order peripheral unitary #

    This file constructs the Fourier spectral projections attached to a unitary of finite order and states their cyclic behavior under a linear map.

    Main statements #

    • exists_cyclic_projections_of_peripheral_unitary — the cyclic projection family associated with a finite-order peripheral unitary.

    theorem exists_cyclic_projections_of_peripheral_unitary {D m : ℕ} [NeZero m] (T : MatrixEnd D) {γ : ℂ} (hγprim : IsPrimitiveRoot γ m) (U : ↥(Matrix.unitaryGroup (Fin D) ℂ)) (hUm : ↑U ^ m = 1) (hPow : ∀ (k : ℕ), T (↑U ^ k) = γ ^ k • ↑U ^ k) :
    ∃ (P : Fin m → MatrixAlg D), (∀ (k : Fin m), IsOrthogonalProjection (P k)) ∧ ∑ k : Fin m, P k = 1 ∧ ↑U = ∑ k : Fin m, γ ^ ↑k • P k ∧ ∀ (k : Fin m), T (P (k + 1)) = P k

    Spectral projections of a finite-order peripheral unitary.

    Here γ should be thought of as the canonical phase exp(2π i / m), represented in Lean by an abstract primitive root hγprim : IsPrimitiveRoot γ m.
