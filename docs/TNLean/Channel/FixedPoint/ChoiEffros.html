    Choi--Effros identities from fixed-point multiplicativity #

    In this file we prove a fixed-point-algebra form of the Choi--Effros projected-product identities for an idempotent unital Kraus map whose adjoint map has a positive definite fixed point.

    Main results #

    • Kraus.choi_effros_left: if the left factor is already projected, then projecting the right factor does not change the final projected product.
    • Kraus.choi_effros_right: the symmetric statement for the right factor.
    • Kraus.choi_effros_sandwich: the product of two projected factors is itself fixed.
    • Kraus.choi_effros_left_eq_right: the left and right projected-product formulas agree.

    The proof is short: idempotence makes E(X) and E(Y) fixed points, Wolf Theorem 6.12 places fixed points in the multiplicative domain, and the multiplicative-domain identities from Chapter 5 give the projected-product formulas.

    This is the version needed later for projected multiplications on ranges of idempotent maps. The more general completely positive contractive projection theorem is not formalized here.
