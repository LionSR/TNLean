    Trace of the continuous functional calculus on a Hermitian matrix #

    Small helper lemmas used in the trace_rpow_* convexity/concavity proofs: the trace of hA.cfc f equals the sum of f over the eigenvalues of A.

    The proof combines the unitary-diagonal form of Matrix.IsHermitian.cfc (see Mathlib.Analysis.Matrix.HermitianFunctionalCalculus) with Matrix.trace_mul_cycle and Matrix.trace_diagonal, exactly the pattern used by Matrix.PosSemidef.trace_eq_zero_iff in Mathlib.

    source
    theorem Matrix.IsHermitian.trace_cfc_eq_sum {n : Type u_1} {𝕜 : Type u_2} [RCLike 𝕜] [Fintype n] [DecidableEq n] {A : Matrix n n 𝕜} (hA : A.IsHermitian) (f : ℝ → ℝ) :
    (hA.cfc f).trace = ∑ i : n, ↑(f (hA.eigenvalues i))

    The trace of hA.cfc f is the sum of f over the eigenvalues of A.

    This is the standard calculation trace (U * diagonal (f ∘ λ) * U†) = ∑ i, f (λ i) obtained from the spectral theorem and trace_mul_cycle.
