    Rectangular span universality auxiliary lemmas: nilpotent-index growth #

    This module contains the Section 8f½ growth lemmas for the rectangular spans attached to the nilpotent-index power (A i₀)^r, where r = nilpIndex (toLin' (A i₀)).

    Section 8f½: NilpIndex growth infrastructure #

    The growth infrastructure in Section 8 (left-step membership, injectivity, finrank monotonicity, surjectivity) was proved for P = (A i₀)^D. Here we prove the analogous results for P = (A i₀)^r where r = nilpIndex.

    The key observation: since range((A i₀)^r) = range((A i₀)^D) (by range_pow_eq_of_nilpIndex_le), the Fitting disjointness ker(A i₀) ∩ range((A i₀)^r) = {0} follows from the D-th power version.

    This enables removing the hMono hypothesis from the strict-growth theorems in Section 8g, closing the gap between the proved monotonicity (for (A i₀)^D) and the needed monotonicity (for (A i₀)^r).
