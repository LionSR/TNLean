\chapter{Renormalization Fixed Points for Matrix Product Density Operators}\label{ch:mpdo_rfp}

Starting from the MPO, MPDO, and LPDO notation of Chapter~\ref{ch:mpdo},
this chapter compares the zero-correlation-length and renormalization
fixed-point conditions used for density-operator chains. The comparison passes
through the physical-trace transfer $\mathcal T_M$, the doubled-index transfer
map $\E_M$, and fixed-point structures obtained from local purifications.
